# Distribution Channels Segment

This segment organizes the market by sales and distribution channels.

## Channels

### Supermarkets/Hypermarkets
Directory: `supermarkets-hypermarkets/`

Large-format retail stores with extensive beauty sections. Characteristics:
- Wide product selection
- Competitive pricing
- One-stop shopping convenience
- High foot traffic
- Multiple brands available

### Convenience Stores
Directory: `convenience-stores/`

Smaller format stores offering essential skincare options. Characteristics:
- Limited but essential product selection
- Easy accessibility
- Extended operating hours
- Quick purchase convenience
- Neighborhood locations

### Specialist Stores
Directory: `specialist-stores/`

Dedicated beauty and skincare specialty retailers. Characteristics:
- Expert staff and consultations
- Curated product selections
- Premium brands focus
- In-depth product knowledge
- Personalized service

### Online Retail
Directory: `online-retail/`

E-commerce platforms, brand websites, and digital marketplaces. Characteristics:
- Convenience of home delivery
- Wide product range
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Competitive pricing
- 24/7 availability
- Subscription options

### Other Distribution Channels
Directory: `other-channels/`

Alternative distribution methods including:
- Pharmacies
- Salons and spas
- Professional channels
- Direct selling
- Pop-up stores
- Beauty subscription boxes

## Purpose

This segmentation helps analyze distribution strategies, channel preferences, and enables optimization of go-to-market approaches across different retail formats.
