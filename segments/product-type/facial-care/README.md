# Facial Care Products

Products designed to remove dirt, oil, and impurities from facial skin, maintain skin moisture, and provide targeted treatments.

## Product Categories

### Cleansers
Products designed to remove dirt, oil, and impurities from facial skin.

### Moisturizers and Creams
Hydrating products designed to maintain skin moisture and barrier function.

### Serums and Essence
Concentrated treatments formulated with active ingredients for targeted skin concerns.

### Toners
Products that help balance skin pH and prepare skin for subsequent treatments.

### Face Masks
Treatment products applied to the face for specific skincare benefits.

### Other Facial Care Products
Additional facial treatments and specialized care products not covered in the main categories.

## Market Relevance

Facial care products represent a significant portion of the skincare market, addressing various skin concerns from basic cleansing to advanced anti-aging treatments.
