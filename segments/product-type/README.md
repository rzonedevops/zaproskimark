# Product Type Segment

This segment categorizes the market by product types.

## Categories

### Facial Care Products
Directory: `facial-care/`

Products designed for facial skincare, including:
- Cleansers
- Moisturizers and creams
- Serums and Essence
- Toners
- Face Masks
- Other Facial Care Products

### Body Care Products
Directory: `body-care/`

Products formulated for body skincare, including:
- Body Lotion
- Foot and Hand Cream
- Other Body Care Products

### Lip Care Products
Directory: `lip-care/`

Products specifically formulated for lip care and protection.

## Purpose

This segmentation helps organize products based on their intended application area on the body, enabling targeted analysis and product categorization.
