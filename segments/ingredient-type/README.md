# Ingredient Type Segment

This segment differentiates products based on their ingredient composition.

## Types

### Conventional
Directory: `conventional/`

Products formulated with traditional synthetic ingredients. Characteristics include:
- Scientifically developed synthetic compounds
- Stable and consistent formulations
- Often more affordable
- Wider availability
- Proven efficacy through clinical testing
- May include parabens, sulfates, and other synthetic preservatives

### Natural/Organic
Directory: `natural-organic/`

Products made with naturally-derived and organic ingredients. Characteristics include:
- Plant-based and botanical ingredients
- Organic certification (in many cases)
- Minimal synthetic additives
- Environmentally conscious formulations
- Free from harsh chemicals
- May appeal to health-conscious consumers
- Often sustainably sourced

## Purpose

This segmentation addresses the growing consumer preference for natural and organic products while maintaining representation of conventional formulations. It enables analysis of market trends related to ingredient preferences and sustainability concerns.
