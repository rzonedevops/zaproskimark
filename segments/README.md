# Market Segments

This directory contains the organized market segmentation structure for the South African Skincare Product Market.

## Segment Directories

### 1. Product Type
Directory: `product-type/`

The market categorized by product types:
- **Facial Care Products**: Products designed for facial skincare
- **Body Care Products**: Products for body skincare
- **Lip Care Products**: Products specifically for lip care

### 2. Category
Directory: `category/`

Market divided by price point and positioning:
- **Mass**: Affordable products for broad consumer base
- **Luxury/Premium**: High-end premium market products

### 3. End User
Directory: `end-user/`

Market segmented by target demographics:
- **Men**: Products for male consumers
- **Women**: Products for female consumers
- **Kids/Children**: Products for children's sensitive skin

### 4. Ingredient Type
Directory: `ingredient-type/`

Products differentiated by ingredient composition:
- **Conventional**: Traditional synthetic ingredients
- **Natural/Organic**: Naturally-derived and organic ingredients

### 5. Distribution Channels
Directory: `distribution-channels/`

Market organized by sales channels:
- **Supermarkets/Hypermarkets**: Large-format retail stores
- **Convenience Stores**: Smaller format essential stores
- **Specialist Stores**: Dedicated beauty and skincare retailers
- **Online Retail**: E-commerce and digital platforms
- **Other Channels**: Alternative distribution methods

## Usage

Each segment directory contains subdirectories for specific categories within that segment. Navigate to the appropriate directory to explore or add data related to that market segment.
