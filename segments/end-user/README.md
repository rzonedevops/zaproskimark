# End User Segment

This segment categorizes the market by target consumer demographics.

## Segments

### Men
Directory: `men/`

Products specifically formulated and marketed for male consumers. These products often address:
- Men's specific skincare concerns
- Masculine fragrances and packaging
- Post-shave care needs
- Facial hair care integration

### Women
Directory: `women/`

Products designed for female consumers. These products typically focus on:
- Women's specific skincare needs
- Anti-aging and beauty enhancement
- Feminine aesthetic preferences
- Comprehensive skincare routines

### Kids/Children
Directory: `kids-children/`

Gentle formulations suitable for children's sensitive skin. These products feature:
- Mild, hypoallergenic ingredients
- Gentle formulations
- Tear-free options
- Pediatrician-tested safety
- Child-friendly packaging

## Purpose

This segmentation helps understand demographic-specific market trends, product preferences, and allows for targeted marketing and product development strategies.
