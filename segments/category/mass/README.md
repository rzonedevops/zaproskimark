# Mass Market Skincare

Affordable skincare products accessible to a broad consumer base.

## Characteristics

### Pricing Strategy
- **Competitive pricing**: Products priced to be accessible to mainstream consumers
- **Value for money**: Focus on delivering effective results at affordable price points
- **Volume-driven sales**: Business model based on high sales volumes

### Market Position
- **Wide availability**: Distributed through multiple retail channels
- **Mainstream appeal**: Products designed for general consumer needs
- **Brand recognition**: Often backed by established consumer goods companies

### Product Features
- **Essential skincare**: Focus on fundamental skincare needs (cleansing, moisturizing, protection)
- **Simple formulations**: Effective ingredients without premium additives
- **Practical packaging**: Functional and cost-effective packaging design
- **Larger pack sizes**: Often available in family or value sizes

### Target Audience
- **Middle-income consumers**: Primary focus on mainstream income segments
- **Value-conscious shoppers**: Consumers seeking quality at accessible prices
- **Families**: Products suitable for daily household use
- **First-time skincare users**: Entry-level products for skincare beginners

## Market Relevance

Mass market skincare products form the foundation of the South African skincare market, providing essential skincare solutions to the majority of consumers. This segment drives volume sales and market penetration, making quality skincare accessible to a broad demographic.

## Distribution

Mass market products are typically found in:
- Supermarkets and hypermarkets
- Convenience stores
- Chain pharmacies
- Online retail platforms
- General merchandise stores
