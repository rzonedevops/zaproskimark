# Category Segment

This segment divides the market by price point and positioning.

## Categories

### Mass
Directory: `mass/`

Affordable skincare products accessible to a broad consumer base. These products are typically:
- Competitively priced
- Widely available
- Targeted at mainstream consumers
- Focused on essential skincare needs

### Luxury/Premium
Directory: `luxury-premium/`

High-end skincare products positioned in the premium market segment. These products typically feature:
- Premium pricing
- High-quality ingredients
- Exclusive formulations
- Sophisticated packaging
- Targeted at affluent consumers

## Purpose

This segmentation enables analysis of market dynamics across different price tiers and helps understand consumer preferences based on purchasing power and brand perception.
